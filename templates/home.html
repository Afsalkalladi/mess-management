🚀 Getting Started

For Students:

  1. Message @testsaharamessbot on Telegram
  2. Send /start command
  3. Click "📝 Register" to register for mess
  4. Upload payment screenshot when prompted
  5. Wait for admin approval
  6. Get your QR code with /myqr command

For Administrators:

  1. Access the Admin Panel
  2. Review and approve student registrations
  3. Verify payment screenshots
  4. Manage mess cuts and closures
  5. Monitor QR scanner statistics

For Staff:

  1. Access the QR Scanner
  2. Login with your staff token
  3. Scan student QR codes for meal access
  4. View daily statistics
